About Us

"Victoria Medical Centre is a modern healthcare facility dedicated to providing high-quality, patient-centered care. Our team of experienced physicians, nurses, and support staff work collaboratively to offer a wide range of medical services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient. From routine check-ups and preventive care to specialized treatments and advanced diagnostics, we are committed to enhancing health and wellness in our community. At Victoria Medical Centre, we prioritize compassion, innovation, and excellence, ensuring that every patient receives the best care in a comfortable and supportive environment".

Home - Departments

Departments

Cardiology

The cardiology department focuses on diagnosis and treating heart conditions.

Neurology

The neurology department speacilizes in diagnosis and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Gastreontology

The Gastreontology department focuses on the disorders of digestive system, including the issues related to esophagus, stomach, and intestine.

Opthamology

The opthamology department offers diagnosis and treatment of eye conditions and vision correction services.

Kidney Transplant

The Kidney Transplant department offers comprhensive care for patients needing kidney transplants, including evaluation, surgery, and post-operative management

Pediatrics

The pediatrics department provides comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, addressing their physical, emotional, and developmental health needs.
